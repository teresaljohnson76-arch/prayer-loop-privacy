Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 27, 2025

Your spiritual journey is sacred and private. We are committed to protecting your personal information and being transparent about how we handle your data.

1. Information We Collect

Prayer Loop collects the following information to provide our services:

  • Account Information: Email address and username when you create an account
  • Dream Journal Entries: The dreams you record, including titles, descriptions, and AI interpretations
  • Prayer Requests: Your prayers, categories, and status (active/answered)
  • App Usage Data: Anonymous analytics to improve the app experience

2. How We Use Your Information

Your data is used exclusively to:

  • Store and display your personal dream journal and prayer tracker
  • Generate AI-powered dream interpretations using biblical symbolism
  • Identify potential correlations between your dreams and prayers ("The Loop")
  • Track your spiritual milestones and achievements
  • Send you optional morning reminders (with your permission)

3. Data Security

We take your privacy seriously:

  • Encryption: All data is encrypted in transit using TLS/SSL
  • Secure Storage: Your data is stored securely on Supabase servers with row-level security
  • Access Control: Only you can access your dreams and prayers
  • Biometric Lock: Optional Face ID/Touch ID protection for added security

4. AI Processing

When you request AI dream interpretations:

  • Your dream text is sent to our secure backend server
  • The AI processes your dream using biblical symbolism databases
  • Interpretations are stored only in your personal account
  • We do not use your dreams to train AI models
  • No third parties have access to your spiritual data

5. Data Sharing

We do NOT:

  • Sell your personal information to third parties
  • Share your dreams or prayers with anyone
  • Use your data for advertising purposes
  • Allow third-party access to your spiritual journal

6. Your Rights

You have full control over your data:

  • Access: View all your stored data at any time within the app
  • Export: Download your complete spiritual journal via the Share feature
  • Delete: Remove individual entries or request complete account deletion
  • Portability: Export your data in a standard format

7. Data Retention

Your data is retained as long as you maintain an active account. Upon account deletion request, all personal data will be permanently removed within 30 days.

8. Children's Privacy

Prayer Loop is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Effective Date."
